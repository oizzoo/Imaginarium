The Second Earth Universe

  • The idea for Second Earth was born during a writing workshop at SLOTART. It was a turning point for me — I realized that what fascinates me most isn’t just creating fantastical worlds, but asking existential questions through them. I also discovered I didn’t want to answer those questions myself — I wanted to leave that to others.
  • The premise was simple: to find out who we truly are as humanity. What happens when Earthlings meet... other Earthlings? Deep down, what do we really expect from ourselves?
  • I added some reflections on the power of information — how deeply it affects us depending on its source, how complete it is, and how easily (or not) we can be manipulated.
  • Then I spiced it all up with two multi-layered, complex protagonists. What kind of secrets might someone hide when the whole world is watching them? And could the toughest person be a man who despises weapons?
  • Since then, Second Earth has evolved — gaining more nuance, themes, and mysteries. It keeps asking new questions and becoming more tightly interwoven with gameplay ideas. I invite you to explore the materials I’ve prepared — and maybe even answer for yourself: what do *I* expect from Second Earth?
