Hi, my name is Hubert, and I am an aspiring narrative designer

Last year, I completed the Narrative Designer course led by Magdalena Kucenty at SKVOT, where I learned, among other things, how to create characters and non-linear narratives, design quests, integrate story with gameplay mechanics, and work with industry-standard tools. Before that, I spent 15 years organizing scouts camps and LARPs inspired by fantasy worlds. While building these worlds, I combined original or borrowed stories with mechanics modeled on video games, board games, and RPGs, so participants could feel they had a real impact on the story. During the pandemic, leading a team I had assembled, I created a browser game that combined the scouting method with an engaging story and immersive mechanics. As a former forester, I draw on my knowledge of nature to create immersive, living fantasy worlds. I value consistency and coherence above all, because they determine how deeply players can “sink” into a created reality. In my free time, I run D&D campaigns, play major PC titles, and expand my already broad knowledge of Western pop culture. I am fascinated by rich and complex worlds—from Star Wars, through Tolkien and Doctor Who, to the Cthulhu Mythos. What I can bring to projects is worldbuilding consistency, complex characters, and engaging storytelling, because that is what I believe makes a story truly work. I also believe that a strong narrative can sometimes carry weak gameplay, but rarely the other way around.
